# Day 2 – Risk Management in Cloud Security

## What is Risk?

Risk is the possibility that a threat exploits a vulnerability causing harm to an organization.

Risk = Threat × Vulnerability × Impact

## Risk Management Process

1. Risk Identification
2. Risk Analysis
3. Risk Evaluation
4. Risk Treatment

## Risk Treatment Options

Accept
Mitigate
Transfer
Avoid

## Example Cloud Risk

Risk: Public cloud storage exposure

Threat: Unauthorized data access

Vulnerability: Public storage configuration

Impact: Data leakage

Mitigation: Disable public access and enable encryption.

## Risk Register Example

| Risk ID | Description | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|-------|-------------|------------|--------|------------|-----------|
